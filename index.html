PING PONG!

How it works:

This application will take the number you input and returns a range of numbers with the following exceptions:

  • Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "ping"
  • Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with "pong"
  • numbers divisable by 15 are replaced with "pingpong"
